# Notice Requiring Explanation of Non-Registration Reasons - Template

## Legal Basis

According to Article 113 of the Criminal Procedure Law of the People's Republic of China:
> Where the People's Procuratorate believes that the public security organ should have filed a case for investigation but failed to do so, or where the victim believes that the public security organ should have filed a case for investigation but failed to do so and raises an objection to the People's Procuratorate, the People's Procuratorate shall require the public security organ to explain the reasons for non-registration. If the People's Procuratorate believes that the reasons for non-registration provided by the public security organ are untenable, it shall notify the public security organ to file the case, and the public security organ shall file the case upon receiving such notification.

## Notice Format

### Standard Template

```
要求说明不立案理由通知书

[Document Number]

[Recipient Unit Full Name]:

According to Article 113 of the Criminal Procedure Law of the People's Republic of China, please provide a written explanation of the reasons for non-registration of the [Case Name] case to our procuratorate within 7 days of receiving this notice.

                                                                            [Year] Year [Month] Month [Day] Day
                                                                            (Procuratorate Seal)
```

### Document Number Format

- Procuratorate abbreviation + "Jian Xing Bu Li Tong" + [Year] + Sequence Number + "Hao"
- Example: Jing Chao Jian Xing Bu Li Tong [2024] 15 Hao
- Components:
  - "Jing Chao" = Issuing procuratorate abbreviation
  - "Jian Xing Bu Li Tong" = Document type code
  - "[2024]" = Issuance year
  - "15 Hao" = Annual serial number

## Important Notes

1. **Timeliness**: The notice requires the public security organ to respond within 7 days of receipt
2. **Written Form**: Written explanation must be required; verbal responses are not accepted
3. **Evidence Materials**: Public security organs must submit relevant evidence materials simultaneously
4. **Legal Effect**: The notice takes legal effect only after the procuratorate seal is affixed
